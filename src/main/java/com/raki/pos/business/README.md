# Business Management

The Business Management feature allows authorized users to view and maintain the core profile of their business within the system.

## What this feature does

- Displays the current business details of the logged‑in user.
- Shows a simple form with the main business information:
  - Business name
  - Business address
  - Phone number
  - Operational status (Open / Closed)
- Allows the user to update these details when needed.
- Ensures that changes to the business profile are saved and reflected across the application.
- Provides clear feedback when the business information is successfully updated or when something goes wrong.

## When to use it

- When the business name or branding changes.
- When the physical address or contact phone number needs to be updated.
- When the business temporarily closes or reopens and the status must be adjusted.
- Whenever the owner or manager wants to keep the business information accurate for orders, reservations, staffing and reporting.

## Who can access it

- Typically used by business owners, managers and admins.
- Not intended for regular employees or customers.
