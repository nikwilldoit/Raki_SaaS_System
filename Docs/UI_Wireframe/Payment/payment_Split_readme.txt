Payment Split — README (for the attached wireframe)

What the colored quantity chips mean

Blue — “Selected by you”
Shows the quantity you’re about to pay for on this item. Click to change the quantity.

Black — “Available”
Quantity that is unselected and still available for anyone at the table to pick.

Gray — “Locked by others”
Quantity temporarily unavailable because another guest has selected it. It becomes available again if they reduce their selection or cancel before paying.

Green — “Paid” (in the Paid items section)
Quantity that has already been paid and is no longer editable.

How selection works

Click a chip to allocate or adjust the quantity you intend to pay.

If you set the quantity to 0, the item returns to the Unselected (black) state.

Items move to Paid items after a successful payment.

Gray (locked) quantities automatically unlock if the other guest cancels or completes payment.
