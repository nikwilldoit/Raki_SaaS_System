## Key Team Rules

* Holding a meeting every friday on 12pm
* Be punctual to meetings ( Having an Agent, and not waste time)
* Respect each other’s opinions
* Communicate early if you cannot complete a task or delaying
* Stay positive and constructive when giving feedback
* Respect deadlines as much we can
* Preparedness: Come to meetings ready
* Keeping Communication window - Replying fast.

## Key principles governing work distribution

* Divide tasks based on individual strengths (e.g., coding, research, documentation, design).
* Everyone must take part in both technical and non-technical work to ensure fairness.
* Rotating responsibilities for presentations and note-taking.
